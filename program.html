What We Do

TNW Programs are global super connectors for startups and scale-ups, large corporations, governments and funding providers. We blend the four pillars of TNW to boost all actors in the global tech ecosystem.


We shape game-changing tech partnerships, impactful media to drive your brand, flagship events, and networking opportunities with our unmatched intelligence platform and global tech community.


Our ecosystem blends corporates, governments, cities, and cutting edge tech solutions to spark collaborative innovation. Our tailored process enables us to facilitate enduring strategic partnerships with hundreds of businesses every year; Our personal approach makes each one unique to your needs.
